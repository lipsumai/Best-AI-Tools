Auferet is a memory-first AI game master. Most AI storytelling tools lose track of details over a long session; Auferet keeps an Event Library and Character Library and lets you upload your own worldbuilding, so the story stays consistent. Free to play with 10 actions per day, with paid plans for heavier use.
